# **App Name**: Growth OS

## Core Features:

- Growth Command Center: A centralized dashboard providing instant insights into active opportunities, urgent tasks, blocked progress, high-priority items, and recent wins, displayed in clear cards and rows.
- Growth Channel Management: Track and manage all growth opportunities and platforms (e.g., dropshipping, wholesale, partnerships) through configurable Kanban pipeline, detailed table views, and focused priority lists.
- Collaborative Task Management: A shared operational task center for teams, allowing quick addition of tasks, linking to platforms, assigning owners, setting priorities and due dates, with various filtered views.
- Company Resource Library: A central hub for storing and organizing reusable company information such as brand assets, product descriptions, legal documents, and application FAQs to streamline workflows.
- Growth Performance Reporting: Executive-friendly dashboards for management to visualize key growth metrics, including total opportunities, conversion rates by stage, and visibility into high-priority items and bottlenecks.
- AI Growth Assistant: An integrated AI tool that can summarize platform statuses, suggest next actions, identify potential blockers, draft outreach emails, recommend strategic priorities, and summarize weekly team progress.
- User & Permissions Management: Configure user roles (Admin, Manager, Operator, Viewer) with specific access levels to modules and data, ensuring secure and controlled collaboration across the team.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A deep, authoritative purple (#6629CC), providing a strong identity and accent for key interactive elements in a dark theme.
- Background color: A very dark, subtle purplish charcoal (#1F1920), forming the foundation of the premium modern dark interface.
- Accent color: A luminous blue-violet (#6969E3) to highlight calls-to-action, active states, and important information, ensuring clear visibility and modern vibrancy against the dark background.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, slightly techy feel that conveys precision and efficiency, fitting the SaaS aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clear readability across various content types and data displays, ensuring a professional and objective tone suitable for productivity.
- Utilize a consistent set of modern, minimalist icons, preferring stroke-based or subtly filled designs, to enhance navigation and convey information clearly without visual clutter.
- Implement a structured, grid-based layout for displaying data in cards and tabular views, complemented by a persistent sidebar for primary navigation. Emphasis on clear hierarchy, spacious yet efficient content arrangement, and minimal clicks to access essential information.
- Incorporate subtle and performant UI animations for transitions, interactive element feedback (e.g., hover states, selections), and modal entries, providing a polished and fluid user experience that enhances usability without impeding speed.
